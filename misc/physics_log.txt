-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
THE EQUATIONS OF MOVEMENT
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

1. Apply equation every frame
2. In case of framedrops, and in general, goes too fast !
3. average the speed every frame.
4. now thats better !


--------------	Experiment  -----------------------

First: When we just apply the equation every frames:

	//Called every frame (dt = frame delta time)
	m_Velocity += dt * m_Force;
	Transform.Position += dt * m_Velocity;

Second:	When we average the speed every frames:
	glm::vec3 avrg_vel = m_Velocity;
        m_Velocity += dt * m_Force;
        avrg_vel += m_Velocity;
        avrg_vel *= 0.5f;
        Transform.Position += dt * avrg_vel;


with a constant acceleration of 5.0
